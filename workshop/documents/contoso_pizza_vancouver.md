**Description:**
Located in the trendy Gastown district, Contoso Pizza Vancouver celebrates the city's multicultural character and West Coast charm. The rustic-industrial space showcases local artists' work and features a cozy fireplace perfect for rainy Vancouver evenings. Our organic sourdough crust is made with BC-grown grains, and our menu highlights inventive combinations like the "Cedar Smoked Salmon & Dill," "BC Blueberry & Brie," and the "Mountains & Sea" with roasted beets and goat cheese. We serve locally roasted coffee, BC craft beers, and house-made ginger-turmeric sodas. The heated patio overlooks Gastown's historic streets, and we offer board games and cozy seating inside.
